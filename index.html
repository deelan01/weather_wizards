Welcome to The Weather Event Tracker

Select a tab from the sidebar to explore more information on weather events, damages, and trends.

This page focuses on analyzing the impact of hurricanes in terms of wind speeds, pressure levels, fatalities, and economic losses.

It provides insights into historical hurricane patterns, trends, and regional impacts using PostgreSQL data.

The project includes interactive visualizations using ECharts, such as radar charts, to compare key metrics like wind speed, pressure, and casualties.

Our goal is to empower better understanding of weather patterns, risk management, and preparedness strategies through visual dashboards.
